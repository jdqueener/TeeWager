How it works

Golf's friendliest wager,
finally tracked properly.

A "bean" is your group's wagering currency. Set the value before you tee off — every achievement earns or costs beans. Settle up at the 19th hole.

💵
Set the value
Choose a dollar amount per bean before the round — $0.25, $1, $5. Your call, your stakes.
🫘
Earn beans on every wager
Tap to award beans as each wager is won — birdie, KP, long drive, sandy par, and more. The player who wins the wager earns the beans.
🏌️
Play 18 holes
Track every hole. The leaderboard updates in real time so everyone knows who's winning.
🍺
Settle up
One tap shows exactly who pays who and how much. Share the card to the group chat.
